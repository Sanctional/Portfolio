01 / CASE STUDY

Auditor Scheduling & Data Integration

WORKFLOW · SCHEDULING
Context

A large operations team ran recurring audit sessions across multiple sites. Schedules lived in scattered spreadsheets; assignments were copy-pasted between owners weekly.

Problem

Manual scheduling created mismatched assignments, late confirmations, and an estimated 30% rate of last-minute swaps that disrupted sessions.

Solution

Designed a single source of truth in Google Sheets — a master roster with validation rules, role-based assignment templates, and a derived view that summarised the upcoming week. Standardised intake from team leads via a simple form so changes flowed into one place.

Tools
Excel Google Sheets Scheduling Operations
Data scale

350+ sessions / month · 1,000+ records / month

Impact
  • ~20% improvement in scheduling efficiency
  • ~30% reduction in session disruptions
  • Lead time for schedule publication cut from 3 days to same-day
02 / CASE STUDY

Audit Tracking & Compliance Workflow

COMPLIANCE · REPORTING
Context

Audit status was tracked inconsistently across leads. Compliance reviews required manual collation and reconciliation each cycle.

Problem

Reports took half a day to assemble and contained drift between sources, leading to follow-up loops and missed deadlines.

Solution

Built a unified tracking schema in Excel with categorical status fields, owner, due date, and resolution notes. Used pivot tables and conditional formatting for weekly rollups, with a one-page summary view leads could filter by team and cycle.

Tools
Excel Google Sheets Compliance Tracking Reporting
Data scale

Reviewed across 6 teams · weekly cycle

Impact
  • Compliance reporting time reduced from ~4 hours to ~30 minutes
  • Eliminated duplicate-entry drift between team sheets
  • Audit close-out rate improved across consecutive cycles
03 / CASE STUDY

Data Cleaning & Standardisation Workflow

PIPELINE · STANDARDISATION
Context

Operational inputs arrived in inconsistent formats — different column names, mixed casing, partial dates, free-text categories.

Problem

Each downstream report needed ad-hoc cleanup, doubling effort and introducing transcription errors.

Solution

Built a structured Sheets workbook with column-rename maps, date-format helpers, and a category normalisation lookup table. Documented a checklist so non-engineers could re-run the workflow on each new input batch.

Tools
Excel Google Sheets Data Cleaning Data Validation
Data scale

~1,000 records / month · 8 input variants

Impact
  • Estimated 40% reduction in manual workload for downstream reports
  • Deduplicated category list from ~60 freeform values to 14 canonical ones
  • Reused across three subsequent reporting projects
04 / CASE STUDY

Operations Reporting & Tracker Improvements

REPORTING · OPERATIONS
Context

An existing report surfaced raw metrics but no narrative — leads still asked the same week-over-week questions in meetings.

Problem

The report answered "what?" but not "what changed?" — defeating the point of self-serve reporting.

Solution

Restructured the tracker in Excel into three views: a week-over-week summary, a rolling 8-week trend, and an exception page surfacing outliers. Added clear callouts and conditional formatting for material changes.

Tools
Excel Google Sheets Reporting Operations Analytics
Data scale

Used by ops leads across multiple teams · weekly cadence

Impact
  • Reduced standing-meeting time discussing report basics
  • Leads began acting on outlier flags within the same week
  • Tracker adoption rose across the team after redesign
